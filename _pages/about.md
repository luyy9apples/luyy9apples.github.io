I am a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), expected to graduate in 2026. My earlier research focused on NLP foundations, particularly text representation learning. My current research lies at the intersection of Trustworthy AI, LLM Reasoning, and Knowledge Graphs, exploring the forms of knowledge and mechanisms of reasoning in modern AI. Ultimately, I aim to investigate the fundamental issue of aligning human cognition with AI cognition.

## Research Interests

- **Trustworthy AI** — uncertainty quantification, neural-symbolic AI
- **LLM Reasoning** — retrieval-augmented reasoning, reasoning mechanisms
- **Knowledge Graph** — logical reasoning, fuzzy inference
